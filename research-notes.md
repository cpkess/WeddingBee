# Venue Research Notes — Gabrielle & Kris, Summer/Fall 2028

## 1. Castle Farms — Charlevoix, Michigan (Fairytale Castle)
- 1918 French-inspired estate (built as Loeb Farms by Albert Loeb, acting pres. of Sears). Stone walls, turrets, courtyards, formal gardens. Restored from ruin starting 2001.
- Capacity up to ~300–350 guests; four/five distinct reception spaces.
- Reception rooms: **King's Great Hall** (iron chandeliers, balcony views), **East Garden Room** (bright/airy "blank canvas," trellis ceiling, French doors to private garden), **West Garden Room** (wainscoting, white chandeliers — "most romantic"), **Knight's Castle** (original stone, dramatic chandelier — premier option).
- Ceremony courtyards: **Queen's Courtyard** (brick walkways, soaring stone walls, five Romanesque arches), **Knight's Courtyard** (soaring stone arch, crenellations, gazebo & fountain). Indoor option: King's Indoor Chapel / King's Gallery for off-season.
- Included in site fee: tables, chairs, white linens, candle centerpieces, dedicated venue coordinator, on-site bridal dressing room. Choose your own vendors. 1918 Cellars house wine required for wine service (BYO beer/liquor ok).
- Signature: dragon statue / "dragon hunt," Michigan's largest outdoor model railroad, gardens. Near resort town of Charlevoix (boating, wineries). Site fee approx $5,500–$12,000.

## 2. Beaches Turks & Caicos — Grace Bay Beach, Providenciales (Family Vacation Paradise)
- All-inclusive, family-friendly. Six themed "villages," each own lobby/pools/style.
- 45,000 sq ft water park, 10 pools, 20+ restaurants, 15 bars, 2 Red Lane Spas, Kids Camp (infants–teens), adults-only pool. 15 min from airport; sits on 12-mile Grace Bay (voted world's best beach).
- Ceremony venues: beach, oceanfront gazebo, garden gazebo, Schooner Sunset Wedding Deck, Seaside Serenade Gazebo, Garden of Love.
- Reception venues: Key West Village, Beachfront Gazebo, Pavilion, Ballroom.
- WeddingMoons packages (planning, catering, photography, spa). Often a WeddingMoons credit for extended stays. Snorkeling, watersports.

## 5. Sandos Finisterra — Cabo San Lucas (Clifftop Adventure)
- "Finisterra" = "End of the Earth." Set on a dramatic clifftop at the tip of the Baja Peninsula, near Land's End Arch. Panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean, Sea of Cortez, and the Cabo marina.
- 7 acres of golden-sand beach (ocean not safe for swimming — gorgeous for ceremonies/strolls). 3 pools: lagoon-style main, adults-only with swim-up bar, VIP pool (Finisterra Suites).
- Ceremony/reception venues: beachfront, elegant desert garden, mountaintop terrace (panoramic clifftop). Sunsets guaranteed dreamy. All-inclusive.
- "Dreamcatcher" wedding package; packages include cake, flowers, photography, setup, dedicated wedding team. Named Top 10 Best All-Inclusive Resorts in Mexico (Travel & Leisure). IGLTA-affiliated.
- Activities: tequila tasting, salsa lessons, sailing/kayaking/windsurfing, sea-turtle swims, camel safaris, luxury yacht cruises, casino nights, nightly stage shows. Spa del Mar built into the mountainside. Steps from downtown Cabo & marina.
